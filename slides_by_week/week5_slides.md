**Day 21: Web Search and Deep Research**

*What was covered:*
- Topic: alternative fuel pathways in maritime (ammonia, methanol, LNG)
- Step 1 — web search: structured five-dimension comparison, executive summary, and comparison table with source citations
- Step 2 — judgment layer: trade-offs, risks, and uncertainties per fuel in five bullets; Sierra frames for decision support
- Step 3 — deep research: full technical report with detailed citations, downloadable, reusable for briefings and client materials
- Bottom line: LNG most operationally established, methanol has near-term momentum, ammonia has long-term potential but significant hurdles
- Sierra links to evidence throughout — outputs are a starting point for review, not finished conclusions
